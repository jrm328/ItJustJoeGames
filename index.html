About Us

Its Just Joe Games is dedicated to cloud gaming, indie development, and tech innovation. We keep gamers and tech enthusiasts at the forefront with insightful content.

Out of Mana

Out of Mana is a fast-paced 2D top-down bullet hell shooter where a wizard, stripped of his powers, has to fight through hordes of enemies using nothing but brute force. Players navigate through elemental dungeon levels, battling unique enemies and bosses with increasingly wild weapons, all while unraveling a mystery that hints at betrayal. It’s magic meets mayhem in a quirky, action-packed adventure.
